Senior Checklist (Before End of Junior Year)

Academics

Maintain strong GPA and pass all core classes
Meet with counselor to review graduation requirements
Choose rigorous junior/senior courses that match your goals

College & Career

Create a college list (reach, match, safety)
Start a résumé with activities, awards, and skills
Research scholarships and note major deadlines
Plan for SAT/ACT or decide test-optional strategy

Extracurriculars & Leadership

Commit to a few meaningful clubs or activities
Seek or prepare for leadership roles
Track volunteer hours and impact

Personal & Summer

Create a professional email and clean up social media
Plan summer: job, internship, program, or volunteering
